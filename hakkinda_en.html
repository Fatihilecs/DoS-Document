About

Dominion of States is a strategy game that combines state management, diplomacy, economy, and war strategies. It also offers alternative scenarios and mod support.

Players choose from pre-existing scenarios and pick a country within that scenario. Then they try to dominate the world through diplomacy, economy, military strategies, and ideologies.

Warning: Dominion of States Beta and Dominion of States (Full Version) are different games. The Beta version is being developed to prove the game is real and to receive donations and investments. The Full Version will feature constant major updates, exclusive mechanics not found elsewhere, flawless experience on mobile devices, and many never-before-seen innovations.

1. General Game Information

  • Game Name: Dominion of States Beta
  • Platforms: Mobile and PC
  • Genre: Strategy, Diplomacy, Management, Economy, 2D, Grand Strategy
  • Target Audience: Players interested in strategy games (9+)

2. Game Mechanics

2.1. General Gameplay

  • Objective: Players choose a country within a selected scenario and aim to dominate the world using diplomacy, economy, military strategy, and ideologies.
  • Game Structure: RTS (Real-Time Strategy) gameplay.

2.2. Core Mechanics

  • Diplomacy: Form alliances, sign trade deals, declare wars or make peace agreements with other nations.
  • Management: Balance the economy, manage population and development policies, taxation.
  • Military Strategy: Prepare for wars by building armies and capturing strategic zones.
  • Ideologies: Players can select ideologies that shape their relationships with other states.

2.3. Buildings and Development

Players can construct various buildings in cities to boost their economy and military power. Example buildings include factories, education centers, military bases, ports (more to be added).
