Overview

Game Name: Dominion of States Beta

Genre: Strategy Diplomacy Management Economy 2D Grand Strategy

Target Audience: Players interested in strategy games (9+)

Short Description

Dominion of States is a strategy game that brings together state management, diplomacy, economy, and warfare, offering alternate scenarios and mod support.

Similar Games: Age of History 3, Europa Universalis IV, Hearts of Iron 4

Important Notice

Dominion of States Beta and Dominion of States (Full Version) are different games. The Beta version is being developed to prove the game is being made and to attract donations and investment. The full version will feature continuous major updates, exclusive mechanics, a smooth experience on mobile devices, and many new innovations.

Players choose one of the existing scenarios based on their preferences and select a country within the scenario to control. They aim to dominate the world through diplomacy, economy, military strategies, and ideologies.

‚öôÔ∏è Game Mechanics

General Gameplay

Objective: Players select a scenario and a country within it, then strive to dominate the world through diplomacy, economy, military strategies, and ideologies.

Game Structure: RTS (Real-Time Strategy) gameplay.

Main Mechanics

  • Diplomacy: Form alliances, make trade agreements, declare wars, or sign peace treaties with other states.
  • Management: Balance the economy, implement taxation, manage population, and plan development policies.
  • Military Strategy: Build armies, prepare for war, and control strategic regions.
  • Ideologies: Choose ideologies that shape relations with other countries.

Buildings and Development

Players can construct various buildings in their cities to increase economic and military power. These include factories, education centers, military bases, ports, etc. (more to be added).
